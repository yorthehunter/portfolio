Summary

I aspire to improve the experiences of others through a foundation of empathy, observation, and reaction. This often manifests through web and mobile user interfaces, but can also be in strong interpersonal relationships.

TL;DR: I like to make people happy.

What else do I do with ALL of my free time??

I have two young boys that wreck and thrash and scream and help make my life whole. As an experience designer that depends on using empathy as a tool, it's so nice to have these two little guys to help me explore the farthest stretches of emotion.

I also am relentlessly and openly nostalgic. I love 80s action movies, I collect retro boxed video games, and I live in an old victorian house full of antiques. Nope, these things don't necessarily make me a better designer.

I've been designing things for years, even in the physical world (I almost wrote "analog world"). I sometimes draw charcoal portraits and every once-in-a-while I may do some digital artwork and very infrequently I may do some watercolor or pen work. I also love to work on my old house: painting walls, stripping baseboards, and hanging drywall.
